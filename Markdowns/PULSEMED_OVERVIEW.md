## The Three-Pillar Architecture

### Pillar 1: Physician-Curated Knowledge Base

- Physicians upload their own documents (PDFs, protocols, handouts)
- System extracts text automatically (including OCR for scanned documents at 91%+ accuracy)
- AI retrieves ONLY from this approved content
- Every response traceable to source document

**Why It Works:** Eliminates hallucination. Ensures practice-specific accuracy. Full audit trail.

### Pillar 2: Progressive Learning

- System learns from patient interactions
- Common questions and patterns identified
- New knowledge proposed to physicians for approval
- Quality metrics continuously monitored (<2% hallucination target, >80% citation coverage)

**Why It Works:** System gets smarter over time while physicians maintain quality control.

### Pillar 3: Humanistic Interaction

- AI trained for emotionally appropriate, patient-friendly conversation
- Concise answers (2-4 sentences when possible)
- Front-loaded critical information
- Context-aware empathy (acknowledges stress, doesn't overwhelm)
- Temporal awareness (doesn't repeat recent advice)

**Why It Works:** Patients feel heard and understood, not lectured.

### Optional Feature: Algorithmic Triage

- Emergency keyword detection (bypasses AI entirely for 911 situations)
- Severity scoring algorithm
- Escalation protocols (911 → ER → Urgent Care → Office → Educational)
- Available for practices that need it; can be disabled for resource-only chatbots
