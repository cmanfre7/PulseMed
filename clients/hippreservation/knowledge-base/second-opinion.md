## Get a Second Opinion

Unsure about your diagnosis or treatment plan? Our experts are here to help. Submit your information below for a personalized second opinion on your hip condition.

### What to Submit for a Second Opinion

To receive a comprehensive second opinion, you can provide:

- **Previous Orthopedic Physician Notes** - Records from your current or previous orthopedic care
- **MRI Report** - Imaging reports that detail your hip condition
- **X-Ray Images** - Radiographic images of your hip joint
- **Physical Therapy Notes** - Documentation from any PT you've completed
- **Misc./Other** - Any additional relevant medical documentation

### How It Works

1. Fill out the contact form with your basic information
2. Upload your relevant medical records and imaging
3. Provide a description of your condition and concerns
4. Our hip preservation specialists will review your case
5. You'll receive personalized feedback and recommendations

### Why Get a Second Opinion?

A second opinion can help you:

- Confirm your current diagnosis
- Explore alternative treatment options
- Understand if surgery is truly necessary
- Learn about hip preservation procedures you may not have been offered
- Gain confidence in your treatment plan

Our team specializes in complex hip conditions and may offer perspectives or treatment options that weren't previously discussed with you.
