## Our Team

### Dr. Nimesh Patel

Dr. Nimesh B. Patel is a Harvard-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in orthopedic trauma, hip preservation, and total joint reconstruction. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center in Golden, Colorado, where he serves as Chair of Surgery at OrthoColorado Hospital and as a member of the hospital's Medical Executive Committee. From 2015 to 2024, Dr. Patel also served on the Board of Directors at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, contributing to clinical strategy and physician leadership.

Dr. Patel specializes in treating complex fractures and hip preservation conditions, including pelvic and acetabular fractures, malunions, nonunions, and intra-articular fractures. His expertise extends to performing periacetabular osteotomy (PAO) and complex hip reconstructive surgery for patients with hip dysplasia, femoroacetabular impingement (FAI), and other structural abnormalities. He also performs primary and revision hip and knee replacements, restoring mobility and improving quality of life for patients with degenerative joint conditions.

Practicing in Colorado, Dr. Patel frequently treats patients with injuries resulting from the state's active lifestyle — including skiing, rock climbing, biking, and high-energy trauma. His advanced training allows him to manage traumatic injuries involving all areas of the musculoskeletal system, even those complicated by previous joint replacements or fractures.

Dr. Patel takes a personalized and comprehensive approach to care, recognizing that each patient's goals are unique. "Each injury is like its own puzzle," he explains. "The goal is to fit all the pieces together — to restore function, correct alignment, and help patients return to the activities they love."

**Education:**
- Bachelor of Science in Biology from Emory University
- Medical degree at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences
- Residency in orthopedic surgery at the Ochsner Clinic Foundation in New Orleans
- Fellowship in Orthopaedic Trauma at Harvard Medical School's Partners Orthopaedic Trauma Program in Boston

Dr. Patel has published and presented research in the Journal of Pediatric Orthopaedics and Journal of Orthopaedics and has been recognized multiple times as a Top Doctor by 5280 Magazine for excellence in orthopedic surgery and patient care.

Outside of medicine, Dr. Patel enjoys spending time with his wife and four sons. He is an avid soccer player, skier, and outdoor enthusiast who embraces the Colorado lifestyle — from hiking 14ers to experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.

Dr. Patel sees patients through Panorama's Hip Preservation Program in Golden and Westminster.

### Dr. Ronald Hugate

Dr. Ronald R. Hugate is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon and nationally recognized leader in orthopedic oncology, hip preservation, complex joint reconstruction, and limb salvage. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he is known for his innovative approach to treating rare and challenging conditions—including bone cancers, prosthetic reconstructions, and complex deformity corrections that few surgeons undertake.

Dr. Hugate's expertise encompasses advanced surgical techniques in bone tumor resection, limb-saving reconstruction, and complex hip and joint preservation procedures. His background in mechanical engineering provides a deep understanding of biomechanics and implant design, which he applies to developing new solutions for patients requiring highly individualized orthopedic care.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Hugate serves as an Adjunct Associate Professor of Mechanical and Materials Engineering at the University of Denver and as clinical faculty at the Rocky Vista University College of Osteopathic Medicine. His research focuses on osseous and bio-integrated implants, prosthetic innovation, and the biomechanics of limb salvage and reconstruction. He is the author of The Handbook of Hip and Knee Replacement Surgery and has published extensively in peer-reviewed medical journals.

Dr. Hugate is also a Lieutenant Colonel in the U.S. Army Reserves, having deployed four times to combat hospitals in Iraq and Afghanistan. He has been honored twice with the Army Commendation Medal for his service and leadership. Beyond the military, he has participated in multiple medical mission trips, including to Haiti following the 2010 earthquake, providing essential surgical care to underserved communities.

**Education:**
- Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Virginia Tech (Magna Cum Laude)
- Medical degree from Eastern Virginia Medical School
- Surgical internship and orthopedic surgery residency at Penn State University College of Medicine
- Ivins Research Fellowship and Orthopaedic Oncology Fellowship at the Mayo Clinic

Dr. Hugate has been recognized as a 5280 Magazine Top Doctor and received the Douglas Pritchard Award for Outstanding Presentation.

Outside of his professional work, Dr. Hugate lives in the Denver metro area with his family. He enjoys spending time with his children and pursues hobbies including traveling, woodworking, high-performance cars, and car restoration.

### Dr. Michael Ellman

Dr. Michael J. Ellman is a fellowship-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in hip arthroscopy, femoroacetabular impingement (FAI), labral tears, and sports-related hip and knee injuries. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he serves as Director of the Hip Preservation Center and as part of the Denver-based sports medicine team.

Dr. Ellman brings unique experience treating athletes of all ages — from recreational to elite competitors — and currently serves as a consultant team physician for the United States Olympic Ski and Snowboard Teams. His surgical expertise focuses on minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures that address hip impingement, labral tears, and cartilage injuries, helping athletes return to their sport and active lifestyles safely and effectively.

As one of a select group of orthopedic surgeons in the U.S. with advanced training in hip preservation and sports medicine, Dr. Ellman combines innovation, research, and a patient-first approach. His work has been instrumental in refining arthroscopic techniques for the treatment of FAI — conditions that, until recently, were often misdiagnosed or left untreated.

**Education:**
- Bachelor of Science in Biopsychology with Honors and Doctor of Medicine from the University of Michigan
- Orthopedic surgery residency at Rush University Medical Center in Chicago
- Sports medicine and hip arthroscopy fellowship at the Steadman Clinic and Steadman-Philippon Research Institute in Vail, Colorado

Dr. Ellman published over 50 peer-reviewed manuscripts and book chapters during his training. His research earned him the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons Baxter Healthcare Scholarship for outstanding contributions to the field of hip arthroscopy.

Dr. Ellman is a 5280 Magazine Top Doctor and an active member of several professional organizations, including the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons (AAOS), the American Orthopaedic Society for Sports Medicine (AOSSM), the Arthroscopy Association of North America (AANA), and the International Society for Hip Arthroscopy (ISHA).

Outside of medicine, Dr. Ellman enjoys spending time with his wife and three daughters, cheering on the Michigan Wolverines, and embracing Colorado's outdoor lifestyle through hiking, biking, skiing, and basketball.

### Dr. Daniel Haber

Dr. Daniel Haber is a Harvard-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in shoulder, hip, and knee injuries. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he provides advanced care for athletes and active individuals of all ages. Dr. Haber offers both surgical and non-surgical treatments tailored to each patient's unique goals, focusing on minimally invasive and arthroscopic procedures that promote faster recovery and long-term joint health.

Dr. Haber's clinical expertise includes labrum and rotator cuff repairs, total shoulder replacement, ACL reconstruction, patellofemoral stabilization, cartilage restoration, meniscal preservation, and hip arthroscopy for labral tears and impingement. His approach combines state-of-the-art techniques with a personalized care philosophy—prioritizing conservative treatment whenever possible and employing surgical solutions only when necessary to restore optimal performance and function.

Dr. Haber serves as a team physician for the U.S. Men's and Women's Ski and Snowboard Teams and for the University of Denver men's and women's soccer teams. He has also cared for athletes across professional leagues including the NFL, MLB, NHL, NBA, and MLS, and continues to advance sports medicine techniques that help patients return safely to their active lifestyles.

## Our Philosophy

### What We Believe In

At Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, our philosophy is rooted in patient-centered care, specialization, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our team of fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeons—including leaders in hip preservation, trauma, joint reconstruction, and sports medicine—is united by a shared commitment: to help every patient move better, live stronger, and return to the activities they love.

### Personalized Care

Every patient's story is unique. That's why we take time to listen—to understand your goals, lifestyle, and challenges before developing a personalized treatment plan. Whether you're seeking care for hip preservation, orthopedic trauma, sports-related injury, or joint replacement, we tailor every decision to what matters most to you.

### Specialization and Expertise

We believe that true excellence in orthopedics comes through specialization. Each of our surgeons has completed advanced fellowship training in their area of expertise, allowing us to provide cutting-edge, evidence-based care across the full spectrum of musculoskeletal health. This depth of training results in more precise diagnosis, faster recovery, and consistently better outcomes.

### Commitment to Excellence

At Panorama, excellence isn't just a goal—it's our standard. We're constantly advancing through research, innovation, and technology, ensuring that our patients receive the most current and effective treatments available. From minimally invasive surgical techniques to advanced rehabilitation protocols, every aspect of our care is designed to optimize recovery and long-term success.

### A Truly Patient-Centered Approach

We strive to create a welcoming, compassionate environment where your concerns are heard and your care is our priority. Our team is with you every step of the way—from the first consultation through recovery—ensuring that you feel informed, confident, and supported throughout your orthopedic journey.

At Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, our guiding principle is simple: exceptional care starts with exceptional listening. By understanding what matters most to our patients, we help them return to doing what they love—with strength, mobility, and confidence.
