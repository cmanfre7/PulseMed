## Dr. James Genuario, MD

Dr. James Genuario is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon and sports-medicine specialist recognized nationally for his expertise in hip preservation, athletic hip injuries, and minimally invasive arthroscopic surgery. Based in Colorado, he provides advanced surgical and non-surgical care for athletes and active individuals, combining precision, compassion, and innovation to restore mobility and long-term joint health.

### Specializations

Dr. Genuario specializes in the treatment of:

- Femoroacetabular impingement (FAI)
- Labral tears
- Hamstring and gluteal injuries
- Other complex athletic hip conditions
- Hip preservation surgery
- Arthroscopic shoulder and knee procedures
- Regenerative treatments including platelet-rich plasma (PRP) and stem-cell therapy

His goal is to relieve pain, optimize performance, and help patients return to the activities that define their lives.

### Team Physician Roles

Dr. Genuario currently serves as:

- **Head Team Physician** for the Denver Broncos
- **Team Physician** for the Colorado Rockies, Denver Nuggets, and Colorado Rapids
- **Consultant** for the Colorado Ballet

### Education & Training

A Colorado native, Dr. Genuario earned:

- **Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Notre Dame, where he first developed his passion for sports medicine as a collegiate athlete
- **Medical School:** Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine (Alpha Omega Alpha)
- **Residency:** Orthopedic surgery at Dartmouth College (also earned Master's degree in Health Policy)
- **Fellowship:** Sports Medicine and Hip Preservation at the Steadman Hawkins Clinic Denver

### Research & Leadership

Dr. Genuario is an Associate Editor for the American Journal of Sports Medicine, a reviewer for the Orthopaedic Journal of Sports Medicine, and an active researcher dedicated to improving outcomes in cost-effective, evidence-based orthopedic care.

An engaged educator and leader, Dr. Genuario lectures nationally and internationally on advances in sports medicine and hip preservation.
