## Dr. Aaron Casp, MD

Dr. Aaron Casp is a fellowship-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in advanced hip preservation, sports medicine, and arthroscopic surgery. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he provides comprehensive care for patients with hip, shoulder, and knee injuries—from elite athletes to active adults and weekend warriors.

### Specializations

Dr. Casp's clinical expertise includes:

- Hip arthroscopy
- Labral repair
- Periacetabular osteotomy (PAO)
- Ligament reconstruction
- ACL repair
- Shoulder replacement

His focus on hip preservation allows patients to maintain their natural joint and delay or prevent the need for total hip replacement. He is dedicated to restoring function, relieving pain, and helping each patient return to the activities they love with improved strength and mobility.

### Philosophy

A passionate advocate for collaborative, patient-centered care, Dr. Casp believes that great outcomes begin with listening. He works closely with his patients to design individualized treatment plans that align with their goals and lifestyles—whether that involves non-operative management, minimally invasive arthroscopy, or advanced reconstructive surgery.

### Education & Training

- **Undergraduate:** Princeton University (competed as a collegiate wrestler)
- **Medical School:** University of North Carolina School of Medicine
- **Residency:** Orthopedic surgery at the University of Virginia
- **Fellowship:** Sports Medicine and Hip Preservation at the Steadman Philippon Research Institute in Vail, Colorado

### Professional Memberships

Dr. Casp is an active member of several professional organizations, including:

- American Orthopaedic Society for Sports Medicine (AOSSM), where he serves on the Fellowship Committee
- Southern Orthopaedic Association, where he sits on the Board of Directors
- U.S. Ski Team Medical Pool Physician
- American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons (AAOS)
- Gold Humanism Honor Society

### Personal

Outside of medicine, Dr. Casp enjoys skiing, woodworking, exercising, and spending time outdoors with his wife and two young children. He brings the same focus, discipline, and determination to his practice that he once brought to the wrestling mat—helping his patients stay active, strong, and pain-free.

### Practice Locations

Dr. Casp sees patients at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center through the Hip Preservation Program in Golden and Westminster.
