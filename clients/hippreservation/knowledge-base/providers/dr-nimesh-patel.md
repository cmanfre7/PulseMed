## Dr. Nimesh Patel, MD

Dr. Nimesh B. Patel is a Harvard-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in orthopedic trauma, hip preservation, and total joint reconstruction. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center in Golden, Colorado, where he serves as Chair of Surgery at OrthoColorado Hospital and as a member of the hospital's Medical Executive Committee. From 2015 to 2024, Dr. Patel also served on the Board of Directors at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, contributing to clinical strategy and physician leadership.

### Specializations

Dr. Patel specializes in treating complex fractures and hip preservation conditions, including:

- Pelvic and acetabular fractures
- Malunions and nonunions
- Intra-articular fractures
- Periacetabular osteotomy (PAO)
- Complex hip reconstructive surgery for hip dysplasia
- Femoroacetabular impingement (FAI) and other structural abnormalities
- Primary and revision hip and knee replacements

Practicing in Colorado, Dr. Patel frequently treats patients with injuries resulting from the state's active lifestyle — including skiing, rock climbing, biking, and high-energy trauma. His advanced training allows him to manage traumatic injuries involving all areas of the musculoskeletal system, even those complicated by previous joint replacements or fractures.

### Philosophy

Dr. Patel takes a personalized and comprehensive approach to care, recognizing that each patient's goals are unique. "Each injury is like its own puzzle," he explains. "The goal is to fit all the pieces together — to restore function, correct alignment, and help patients return to the activities they love."

### Education & Training

- **Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Biology from Emory University
- **Medical School:** University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences
- **Residency:** Orthopedic surgery at Ochsner Clinic Foundation in New Orleans
- **Fellowship:** Orthopaedic Trauma at Harvard Medical School's Partners Orthopaedic Trauma Program in Boston

### Research & Recognition

Dr. Patel has published and presented research in the Journal of Pediatric Orthopaedics and Journal of Orthopaedics and has been recognized multiple times as a Top Doctor by 5280 Magazine for excellence in orthopedic surgery and patient care.

### Personal

Outside of medicine, Dr. Patel enjoys spending time with his wife and four sons. He is an avid soccer player, skier, and outdoor enthusiast who embraces the Colorado lifestyle — from hiking 14ers to experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.

### Practice Locations

Dr. Patel sees patients through Panorama's Hip Preservation Program in Golden and Westminster.
