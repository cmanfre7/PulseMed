## Dr. Ronald Hugate, MD

Dr. Ronald R. Hugate is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon and nationally recognized leader in orthopedic oncology, hip preservation, complex joint reconstruction, and limb salvage. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he is known for his innovative approach to treating rare and challenging conditionsâ€”including bone cancers, prosthetic reconstructions, and complex deformity corrections that few surgeons undertake.

### Specializations

Dr. Hugate's expertise encompasses advanced surgical techniques in:

- Bone tumor resection
- Limb-saving reconstruction
- Complex hip and joint preservation procedures
- Orthopedic oncology

His background in mechanical engineering provides a deep understanding of biomechanics and implant design, which he applies to developing new solutions for patients requiring highly individualized orthopedic care.

### Academic & Research Roles

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Hugate serves as:

- Adjunct Associate Professor of Mechanical and Materials Engineering at the University of Denver
- Clinical faculty at Rocky Vista University College of Osteopathic Medicine

His research focuses on osseous and bio-integrated implants, prosthetic innovation, and the biomechanics of limb salvage and reconstruction. He is the author of "The Handbook of Hip and Knee Replacement Surgery" and has published extensively in peer-reviewed medical journals.

### Military Service

Dr. Hugate is also a Lieutenant Colonel in the U.S. Army Reserves, having deployed four times to combat hospitals in Iraq and Afghanistan. He has been honored twice with the Army Commendation Medal for his service and leadership. Beyond the military, he has participated in multiple medical mission trips, including to Haiti following the 2010 earthquake, providing essential surgical care to underserved communities.

### Education & Training

- **Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Virginia Tech (Magna Cum Laude)
- **Medical School:** Eastern Virginia Medical School
- **Residency:** Surgical internship and orthopedic surgery at Penn State University College of Medicine
- **Fellowships:** Ivins Research Fellowship and Orthopaedic Oncology Fellowship at the Mayo Clinic

### Recognition

Dr. Hugate has been recognized as a 5280 Magazine Top Doctor and received the Douglas Pritchard Award for Outstanding Presentation.

### Personal

Outside of his professional work, Dr. Hugate lives in the Denver metro area with his family. He enjoys spending time with his children and pursues hobbies including traveling, woodworking, high-performance cars, and car restoration.
