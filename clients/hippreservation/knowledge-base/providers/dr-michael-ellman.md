## Dr. Michael Ellman, MD

Dr. Michael J. Ellman is a fellowship-trained, board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in hip arthroscopy, femoroacetabular impingement (FAI), labral tears, and sports-related hip and knee injuries. He practices at Panorama Orthopedics & Spine Center, where he serves as Director of the Hip Preservation Center and as part of the Denver-based sports medicine team.

### Specializations

Dr. Ellman brings unique experience treating athletes of all ages — from recreational to elite competitors — and currently serves as a consultant team physician for the United States Olympic Ski and Snowboard Teams. His surgical expertise focuses on:

- Minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures
- Hip impingement treatment
- Labral tears repair
- Cartilage injuries
- Sports-related hip and knee injuries

As one of a select group of orthopedic surgeons in the U.S. with advanced training in hip preservation and sports medicine, Dr. Ellman combines innovation, research, and a patient-first approach. His work has been instrumental in refining arthroscopic techniques for the treatment of FAI — conditions that, until recently, were often misdiagnosed or left untreated.

### Education & Training

- **Undergraduate & Medical School:** Bachelor of Science in Biopsychology with Honors and Doctor of Medicine from the University of Michigan
- **Residency:** Orthopedic surgery at Rush University Medical Center in Chicago
- **Fellowship:** Sports medicine and hip arthroscopy at the Steadman Clinic and Steadman-Philippon Research Institute in Vail, Colorado

### Research & Recognition

Dr. Ellman published over 50 peer-reviewed manuscripts and book chapters during his training. His research earned him the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons Baxter Healthcare Scholarship for outstanding contributions to the field of hip arthroscopy.

Dr. Ellman is a 5280 Magazine Top Doctor and an active member of several professional organizations, including:

- American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons (AAOS)
- American Orthopaedic Society for Sports Medicine (AOSSM)
- Arthroscopy Association of North America (AANA)
- International Society for Hip Arthroscopy (ISHA)

### Personal

Outside of medicine, Dr. Ellman enjoys spending time with his wife and three daughters, cheering on the Michigan Wolverines, and embracing Colorado's outdoor lifestyle through hiking, biking, skiing, and basketball.
