## Why Pre-Surgery Exercises Matter

Preparing for hip surgery involves more than just mental readiness; strengthening the muscles around your hip joint can enhance recovery, reduce post-operative pain, and improve mobility. Incorporating hip-strengthening exercises before surgery can significantly improve rehabilitation outcomes.

Before starting an exercise routine, consult with your orthopedic specialist or physical therapist to ensure the exercises are safe and effective based on your specific hip condition.
