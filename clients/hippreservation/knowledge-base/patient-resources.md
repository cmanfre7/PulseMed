## Patient Resources

Welcome to the Patient Resources page at HipPreservation.org, your hub for essential information to support your hip health journey. Here, you'll find detailed guides on pre- and post-surgery exercises, recovery tips, and important instructions tailored for your hip preservation surgery. We offer a variety of resources, including exercise protocols, wound care guidance, and postoperative care recommendations to ensure a smooth recovery process. Our goal is to provide you with the tools and knowledge to make informed decisions about your treatment and maintain optimal hip health. Explore our comprehensive materials to stay informed and empowered throughout your recovery process.
